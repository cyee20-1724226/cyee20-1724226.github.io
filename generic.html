Our Mission

...

A love for the outdoors is what inspired us to create Skogen Rec. Through our adventures, we have discovered a deep appreciation for the natural monuments and ecology that surrounds us. Through Skogen, we hope to provide others with the opportunity to embark on their adventures and share in our love for the outdoors.In this spirit, we actively seek to conserve the environments we have come to respect and appreciate.

Skogen Recreational Equipment is a company created by the adventurer, for the adventurer. We are explorers, conservators, and pioneers. We seek to build a healthy and respectful relationship with nature while utilizing the most cutting edge technology to incorporate into our adventures. Together, with our customers and fellow adventurers, we can build a world where technology and nature can live in harmony.
