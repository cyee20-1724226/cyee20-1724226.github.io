About Us

Missions Statement

Founded in 2018, Skogen Recreational Equipment was made in response to the ever-growing fear over climate change. The destruction of our beautiful planet from growing waste, deforestation and carbon emission has brought about concern over how we use our earth’s natural resources. Our founders have made the commitment to combat this issue. Inside us is the need to explore, adventure and push the boundaries that seek to limit us. We believe that through our adventures comes a sense of respect for the earth’s natural beauty. A respect that brings about a sense of guardianship to protect and care for our environment. Here at Skogen Recreational Equipment, we will equip you with the tools needed for this adventure. Our products are sustainably made and built to last so that we reduce the waste that comes with obsolete or broken equipment. Please join us on this adventure. Together we can protect our Skogen (Forests) and become the adventurers we all seek to be.
